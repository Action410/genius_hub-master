# Image Upload Instructions

## Promotional Banner Images

Upload your promotional banner images to this `public` folder with the following names:

1. **banner-1.jpg** - Fast Delivery Now Ongoing banner
   - Features: Delivery person flying with cape, "FAST DELIVERY NOW ONGOING", "RELIABLE", "RESPONSIVE", "ORDER NOW"

2. **banner-2.jpg** - Fast & Reliable Delivery banner
   - Features: Delivery driver on red scooter, "FAST & RELIABLE DELIVERY", "QUALITY PRODUCTS", "24/7 SERVICE", "DIRECT TO YOUR DOOR"

3. **banner-3.jpg** - Fast. Secure. Delivered. banner
   - Features: Two delivery drivers, "FAST. SECURE. DELIVERED.", "24/7 SERVICE", vertical layout

4. **banner-4.jpg** - Global Delivery banner
   - Features: Airplane with package, "Delivering to Customers Across the Globe At Affordable Prices", "FAST. SECURE. DELIVERED."

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Recommended Size**: 
  - Banner 1 & 2: 1200x600px (horizontal)
  - Banner 3: 800x1200px (vertical)
  - Banner 4: 1200x600px (horizontal)
- **File Size**: Optimize images to under 500KB for faster loading

## After Uploading

Once you upload the images, the landing page will automatically display them. The placeholders will be replaced with your actual promotional banners.
